Who We Are

A Repair Shop That
Actually Cares.

NM Wireless was built out of frustration. Too many people in Brockville were driving hours to the city for repairs, or paying big-box prices for mediocre work done by someone who'd never see them again.

We wanted to change that. A local shop with premium-grade parts, real technical skills, and a guarantee you can actually hold us to — because we're your neighbours.

"We treat every device like it's our own. That's the standard."

From cracked screens to water-damaged phones that everyone else gave up on — we handle it all, and we do it right the first time.
